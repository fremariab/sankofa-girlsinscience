Career Roadmaps

Explore the five major arenas where science and technology are transforming Ghana.

Science Track

Explore life, matter, energy, and the environment at a fundamental level through lab work, field research, and discovery.

The Synopsis
  • Explores life, matter, energy, and the environment at a fundamental level
  • Combines lab experimentation, fieldwork, and data analysis
  • Powers breakthroughs in agriculture, medicine, climate science, and materials
  • Moves from observation to experimentation to discovery and application
  • Feeds directly into medicine, engineering, and technology innovation

Technology Track

Design and build digital systems that power modern life, from software and AI to networks and data.

Engineering Track

Apply science and mathematics to design and build real systems, infrastructure, and machines at scale.

The Synopsis
  • Applies science and math to design and build systems at scale
  • Combines mechanics, electricity, materials, and energy
  • Drives infrastructure, manufacturing, and industrial growth
  • Moves from problem to design to prototype to build and optimize
  • Bridges theory with real-world construction and production

Medicine / Health Sciences Track

Focus on diagnosing, treating, and preventing disease through patient care, research, and innovation in health systems.

The Synopsis
  • Focuses on diagnosing, treating, and preventing disease
  • Combines biology, chemistry, technology, and patient care
  • Includes clinical practice, research, and public health systems
  • Moves from patient care to research to innovation and systems impact
  • Creates tools, treatments, and systems that save lives at scale

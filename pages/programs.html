Transforming dreams into reality.

Our programs combine mentorship, and practical learning to help girls thrive from school into science careers.

What We Offer

Six pillars of transformation

From one-on-one mentoring to global virtual connections, every program is designed so no girl in science walks alone.

One-on-One Mentoring

Pairing students with professional women in STEM to provide academic insight, career guidance, and communication skills.

Science Resource Center

A fully equipped science library and reading room stocked with up-to-date textbooks and learning support.

Science Clinics

Intensive week-long academic clinics preparing graduating students for final exams and tertiary STEM transition.

Girls in Science Club

A student-led peer mentoring network so program benefits pass to younger year groups and sustain long-term change.

Junior GiS Workshops

Outreach reaching students in Junior High Schools across Central and Greater Accra Regions to broaden STEM exposure.

Global Virtual Mentoring

Connecting Ghanaian students with international professionals, building confidence, communication, and global networks.

Our Partners

Credibility through collaboration

SGiS is made possible through partnerships with global supporters, businesses and schools.

The Experience

Your journey with SGiS

Every girl who walks through our program follows a transformational path from discovery to leadership.

01

Discover

Meet role models and see what is possible in STEM.

Girls meet STEM professionals, and identify pathways that match their strengths and interests.

02

Learn

Access clinics, workshops, and resource support.

Students gain access to personalized career guidance, helping them understand different STEM pathways and make informed decisions about their future. Through interactive workshops, mentorship, and practical learning experiences, they build the skills, confidence, and knowledge needed to pursue their goals.

03

Connect

Build mentorship relationships locally and globally.

Through one-on-one and virtual mentoring, girls build confidence, communication, and long-term professional networks.

04

Excel

Grow confidence and qualify for tertiary STEM pathways.

Participants improve academic results, strengthen leadership habits, and go to university.

05

Lead

Return as alumni mentors and community STEM leaders.

Alumni return to mentor younger girls, run outreach activities, and champion STEM in their schools and communities.

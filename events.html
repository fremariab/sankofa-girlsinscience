Event Recaps

01.

Passing-Out Ceremony — 30 May

The cohort gathered at Airport Residential Area to celebrate graduation. After an opening prayer and welcome by founder Mrs. Nana Beecham, medical student Zaneta Frimpong shared insights from her journey. Henrietta Frimpong encouraged girls to “pay it forward.” Certificates were presented to honor the girls’ dedication and achievements.

02.

Seminar: Self-Knowledge & Awareness

Trainer Ayesha led reflective sessions on self-awareness and goal setting. Girls arrived ready with notebooks, engaged deeply with mentors, and connected over refreshments after the seminar.

03.

Hospital Visit — 2014

42 girls from Breman Asikuma Secondary School visited the maternity ward at Our Lady of Grace Hospital. They presented gifts to new mothers, observed healthcare in action, and learned about careers in public health. The Nurse in Charge thanked the students and encouraged them to pursue healthcare careers.
